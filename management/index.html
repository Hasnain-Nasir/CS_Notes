Syllabus Roadmap

Full course outline from the official syllabus (Robbins & Coulter, 15th ed.).

  • Managers and you in the workplace — management history and the manager's role
  • Four functions of management and Mintzberg's managerial roles
  • Layers of management, required skills, and early management theories
  • Making decisions — process, conditions, biases, and evidence-based decision making
  • Foundations of planning — goals, plans, and contemporary planning issues
  • Managing strategy — strategic management process and competitive strategies
  • Designing organizational structure — mechanistic vs organic design
  • Motivating employees — classical and contemporary motivation theories
  • Being an effective leader — leadership theories and contemporary views
  • Monitoring and controlling — control process and performance measurement
