Syllabus Roadmap

Full course outline from the official syllabus.

  • Overview of technical reporting
  • Use of library and information gathering
  • Administering questionnaires
  • Reviewing gathered information
  • Technical exposition
  • Topical arrangement, exemplification, definition, classification and division
  • Causal analysis and effective exposition
  • Technical narration, description, and argumentation
  • Persuasive strategy
  • Organizing information — brainstorming and organizing material
  • Construction of formal outline and outlining conventions
  • Electronic communication and generating solutions
  • Polishing style — paragraphs and sentence structure
  • Clarity, length, order, and avoiding pomposity
  • Document design — structure, preamble, summaries, and abstracts
  • Table of contents, footnotes, glossaries, and cross-referencing
  • Plagiarism, citation, bibliography, index, and appendices
  • Typesetting systems
  • Creating the professional report (mechanical and graphical elements)
  • Proposals and progress reports
  • Leaflets, brochures, and handbooks
  • Magazine articles and research papers
  • Feasibility reports and project reports
  • Technical research reports
  • Manuals, documentation, and thesis
  • Electronic documents — linear versus hierarchical structure
